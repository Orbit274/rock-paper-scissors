## About

Western based rock paper scissors website <br>
Start a timer and choose between rock, paper, or scissors before it ends

## How to Play

Once on the website, press the ready button to start a timer and let the tumblweed move <br>
Once the tumbleweed reaches the end and stops moving, you will be notified to "DRAW" and have 1 second to pick a choice <br>
The round is over once the second ends and you must compete against the AI <br>
Failure to pick a choice before the 1 second ends results in automatic loss
First to 5 wins
